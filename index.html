Our Story

Where Aviation Expertise
Meets a Love for Travel

"Life is all about making memories… and we're here to help you create them."

Welcome to Air Skylink Travels, where your journey is our passion. Founded in 2009 by industry veteran Jitendra Parihar, our agency was built on a foundation of deep aviation expertise and a profound love for exploration.

Prior to launching Air Skylink Travels, Jitendra dedicated 11 successful years to Jet Airways, gaining invaluable firsthand insight into global airline operations, premium hospitality, and the intricate art of seamless travel management.

We believe travel is far more than moving from one destination to another — it is a collection of beautiful, unforgettable moments that shape our lives. Our mission is to orchestrate the perfect backdrop for your lifelong memories.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your journey with Air Skylink.

How do I book a tour package with Air Skylink Travels?

+
Simply fill out the enquiry form on our website, WhatsApp us at +91 9993237262, or call us directly. Our travel experts will get in touch within 24 hours to understand your preferences and craft a personalised itinerary for you.

Do you provide visa assistance for international destinations?

+
Yes, we provide complete visa assistance for all major international destinations including documentation guidance, application filing support, and appointment scheduling. Our team stays updated with the latest visa requirements and policies.

Can I customise my travel itinerary or are only fixed packages available?

+
Absolutely! We specialise in FIT (Fully Independent Travel) tours where every aspect of your itinerary can be customised — from hotels and flights to sightseeing and dining. Share your preferences and budget, and we'll design a bespoke journey just for you.

What payment options do you accept?

+
We accept all major payment methods including UPI, bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS), credit and debit cards, and cash at our Bhopal office. For international bookings, we also accept wire transfers. EMI options are available on select packages.

Is travel insurance included in the packages?

+
Travel insurance is available as an add-on with all our packages. We highly recommend securing comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and baggage loss. Our team will help you choose the best coverage for your destination.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

+
Cancellation policies vary by destination and season. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations within 15-30 days receive a partial refund. We always try to be flexible and offer rescheduling options whenever possible. Specific policy details are shared with each booking confirmation.

Do you arrange group tours for corporates and large families?

+
Yes! We specialise in corporate travel and MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences & Exhibitions) as well as large family group tours. We handle everything from flight blocks and hotel reservations to local transport, venue bookings, and customised sightseeing for groups of any size.

Are your packages suitable for senior citizens and children?

+
Absolutely. We design age-appropriate itineraries with comfortable pacing, accessible accommodations, and necessary medical assistance. Senior citizen discounts are available on select packages, and we ensure child-friendly activities and safety measures for family trips.
