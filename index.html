Our Story. Our Promise.

Air Skylink Travels was founded in 2009 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh by Jitendra Parihar, a travel professional with 11 years of experience at Jet Airways.

That time in aviation taught us the value of precision, safety, and world-class service. Today, we bring the same standards to every journey we design for you.

From a single dream to hundreds of happy travelers, our journey continues driven by trust, passion, and the love for travel.
