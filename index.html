Our Story

Where Aviation Expertise
Meets a Love for Travel

"Life is all about making memories… and we’re here to help you create them."

Welcome to Air Skylink Travels, where your journey is our passion. Founded in 2009 by industry veteran Jitendra Parihar, our agency was built on a foundation of deep aviation expertise and a profound love for exploration.

Prior to launching Air Skylink Travels, Jitendra dedicated 11 successful years to Jet Airways, gaining invaluable firsthand insight into global airline operations, premium hospitality, and the intricate art of seamless travel management.

We believe travel is far more than moving from one destination to another — it is a collection of beautiful, unforgettable moments that shape our lives. Our mission is to orchestrate the perfect backdrop for your lifelong memories.
